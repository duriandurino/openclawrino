# Report QA Checklist

- cover/classification and version history documented
- engagement overview, scope, exclusions, and ROE documented
- methodology and limitations documented
- executive summary present with overall risk posture and priorities
- findings summary present
- attack paths summarized when chained impact exists
- every finding has evidence, business impact, remediation, and retest guidance
- severity/risk logic explained consistently
- cleanup/restoration section present
- appendices include evidence index and supporting references
- sensitive data redacted
- delivery packaging ready, including report/doc/pdf/slides linkage where applicable
