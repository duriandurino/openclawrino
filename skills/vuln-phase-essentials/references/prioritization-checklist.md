# Prioritization Checklist

Use this order:

1. validated and reproducible?
2. applicable to the exact asset/version/config?
3. exposed or reachable in the threat model?
4. in KEV or other evidence of active exploitation?
5. EPSS / exploitation likelihood available?
6. technical blast radius?
7. business blast radius?
8. compensating controls verified?
9. patch/mitigation feasible?
10. retest plan defined?

## Record per finding
- validation status
- CVSS
- KEV yes/no
- EPSS if available
- internet/internal exposure
- asset owner / criticality
- mitigation path
- retest owner and due date
