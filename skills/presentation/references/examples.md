# Presentation Examples — Real User Triggers

## Example 1: Quick Overview
**User:** "Give me 10 slides for this pentest presentation"
**Expected:** 10-slide deck covering Problem, Methodology, Findings, Remediation, Why OpenClaw

## Example 2: Management Summary
**User:** "Create a 5-slide overview of the findings for leadership"
**Expected:** Executive summary, top 3 risks, business impact, remediation timeline, next steps

## Example 3: Technical Deep-Dive
**User:** "Generate a 15-slide presentation covering the full exploit chain"
**Expected:** Detailed slides per finding with evidence, CVSS, and remediation commands

## Example 4: Custom Topic Focus
**User:** "Create 8 slides focused on the MQTT and fleet security findings"
**Expected:** Slides concentrated on specific findings with business model context

## Example 5: From Report File
**User:** "Turn engagements/pulselink-pi/report/pentest-report-presentation.md into a 12-slide deck"
**Expected:** Slide extraction and restructuring from existing report

## Example 6: With Speaker Notes
**User:** "Give me 7 slides with speaker notes for each slide"
**Expected:** Slide content + talking points the presenter can read

## Example 7: Role-Specific
**User:** "Create a 6-slide deck for non-technical audience"
**Expected:** Simplified language, focus on business impact, minimal jargon
